About Me

Mark Fisher is a freelance web developer who helps small businesses build professional, interactive and user-friendly websites that will stand out to your customer-base, all at a price you can afford.

Before transitioning his career into software development, Mark worked as a Product Implementation Specialist for Realtime Information Technology, Inc. With direct experience working for a small company such as Realtime, Mark understands the needs and demands required to provide the client with a product that exceeds expectations and leaves the customer with a lasting impression.

With Mark's services is the guarantee that you get the product you want with attention to detail, completed in a timely manner with consistent communication throughout all business processes and a proud representation of the brand you work hard for.
